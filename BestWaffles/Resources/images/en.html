The foundation of any mighty empire is a functioning goods distribution network. Make sure that you always have enough raw materials to produce goods to not only keep your empire running smoothly, but to trade for other goods you need as well!

Expand your kingdom by exploring your surroundings and discovering new regions. Be ready to do battle though, bandit camps and other worthy foes will put up a fight to avoid being conquered by you!

Ruling an empire is always more fun with there are friends about! Help your fellow rulers out by chatting in-game to share tips & tricks or trade excess goods with them for those that you need.

As you build your simple plot of land into a mighty empire, you'll encounter many exciting adventures in the world. You'll have to protect your empire from threatening invasions and journey to new islands to expand your rule.
